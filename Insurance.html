Had an accident? What now...

We use all industry standard equipment

1. Call you insurance company

Call your insurance company and inform them of your accident. It may be useful to find out how much your access is on your premium.

2. Get a FREE quote from us

Come and see us where one of our friendly members of staff will take a look at your car and will give us a competive price.

3. Inform your insurance company.

Call your insurance to tell them that you are using us as your car body repairer.

4. Book your repair job in.

Once you've informed your insurance company that you have chosen us as your car body repairer then you can book your car in with us.
