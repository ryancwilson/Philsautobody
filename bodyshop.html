Our Body Shop

Having bodywork damage on your car can be a worrying time but at Phils Auto Body Repairs we will take the worry away and guarantee that by trusting your pride and joy with us was the best decision you could of made. As soon as your car is booked in, dependent on the damage, your car will be stripped and all relevant parts and replacements ordered. At Phils Auto Body Repairs an experienced body repairer will prepare your car for painting. All paints will be matched to industry standards. Once your car has been sprayed it will be allowed to dry in our spray booth at a high temperature for a permanent quality finish. It then goes through quality control to ensure all work carried out has been done to the highest standards. We then wash & polish your car so that you can pick up your 'pride and joy' again.
